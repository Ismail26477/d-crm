# 🚀 AI-Powered CRM & Lead Management System

A full-stack CRM (Customer Relationship Management) system designed to help businesses manage leads, track sales pipelines, and automate follow-ups — all in one place.

---

## 📌 Overview

This project is built to solve real-world business problems by improving sales workflows, reducing manual effort, and increasing conversion efficiency.

It provides a centralized dashboard where teams can manage leads, monitor performance, and streamline operations.

---

## 🎯 Key Features

✅ Lead Management  
- Create, update, and track leads  
- Status tracking (New, Qualified, Proposal, Won, Lost)

✅ Sales Pipeline  
- Drag-and-drop pipeline stages  
- Visual deal tracking  

✅ Follow-up Automation  
- Schedule follow-ups  
- Track overdue activities  

✅ Call & Activity Tracking  
- Log calls and interactions  
- Monitor team performance  

✅ Customer Management  
- Centralized customer database  
- Track deal history  

✅ Reports & Analytics  
- Conversion funnel  
- Performance insights  
- Sales metrics  

✅ Team & Role Management  
- Assign leads to team members  
- Track individual performance  

✅ Integrations Ready  
- Google Sheets  
- Meta Ads  
- WhatsApp (extendable)
